Dennis Rodman
"The Worm"
Dennis Rodman playing for the Chicago Bulls. Rodman's NBA career spanned 14 seasons playing for 5 different teams.

Dennis Keith Rodman is a 2011 Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame inductee who is famous for his fierce defensive and rebounding abilities.

He led the NBA in rebounds per game for 7 consecutive years and won 5 NBA championships.

He earned NBA All-Defensive First Team honors 7 times and won the NBA Defensive Player of the Year Award twice in his career.

He is arguably the best rebounding forward in NBA history.
